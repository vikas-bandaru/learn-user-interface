This is a header

The header typically contains a logo and a navigation menu.

This is a section

A section represents a generic section of content on a page, such as a chapter, a group of related articles, or a single topic within an article.

This is an article

An article represents a self-contained piece of content that could potentially be distributed independently from the rest of the page. It could be a blog post, a news article, a product review, or any other type of content.

This is an aside

An aside represents content that is tangentially related to the main content of the page, such as a sidebar, a pull quote, or an advertisement.

Another section

Sections can contain multiple articles and asides, and can be nested to create a hierarchical structure of content.

Another article

Articles can also contain nested sections, articles, and asides.

A nested section

This is a section nested inside an article.

A nested aside

This is an aside nested inside a section inside an article.

This is a footer

The footer typically contains contact information, copyright notice, and other legal and administrative information.
